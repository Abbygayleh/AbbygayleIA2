HERO

This is Yumeko Jabami, a fictional character from the popular manga and
anime series "Kakegurui," who is noted for her extraordinary gambling
abilities and love of high-stakes gambling. I chose Yumeko as my hero because of the
qualities and traits that she possesses. The traits I look up to the most are:

1. Confidence:
Whenever Yumeko is faced in a situation where she is under great pressure she still remains confident and positive. Being self-assured is one of the traits that can aid you in this life.
2. Critical Thinker:
Yumeko is a great strategist, frequently examining her opponents and their actions to get an advantage. Learning to think strategically can help improve your problem solving and decision making skills.
3. Respectful:
Yumeko demonstrates her respect for others even in a competitive setting and a trait like this is really important in real life because this can foster positive relationships among others.
4. Freethinking:
Yumeko is an independent thinker who frequently trusts her own judgment. While it is crucial to seek advice and collaborate with others, being able to think independently can help you make educated judgments.
