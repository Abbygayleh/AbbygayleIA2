Strongest Emotion

Jovial:
Having a cheerful disposition helps me academically in many ways. Such ways are the following:
  • A cheerful nature frequently draws friends and peers towards. When it comes to studying, group projects, or seeking aid with academics, I am able to turn to these peers that are willing to provide invaluable support and assistance. The same goes with professors.
  • Being a jovial individual also sometimes increases my motivation to learn. I realize that I may be motivated to attend classes, participate in discussions, and complete projects because I see them as possibilities for enjoyment and personal improvement based on what I wish to specialize in.
